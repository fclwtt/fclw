100个漂亮动词，保你英语论文脱颖而出

  • 社会实践
  • 2020-10-19 17:29

当写完论文初稿后，你是否习惯于用更高大上的同义词来对某个单词进行替换？甚至你还可以用一些生动的词来替换掉整个从句？此外，你可能还要适当地多使用主动语态以及一些主动动词，这也能让论文语言结构更丰富。

 

为了让你的论文充满更加生动、更加形象的词汇，从而进一步提升论文的语言层次，本文分类整理了你在撰写或修改英文论文时可能考虑用到的100多个同义词列表，正确使用这些“漂亮”的主动动词，你可以建立起一个更符合英语母语研究者的语境。建议你收藏并仔细阅读，同时别忘了推荐分享给好友一起参考和学习。

 

 

重要说明：

红色标记的部分是最常用的表达，但我们不推荐过多使用，请尽量使用合适的同义词替换掉它们，使你的论文语言更生动。

 

1描述研究范围                                

 

1.1表达论文的研究目的

 

[This paper/ study/ investigation…]

·        applies

·        explores

·        aims at

[Prior research/ investigations…]

·        surveys

·        questions

·        highlights

·        outlines

·        features

·        investigates

·        discusses

·        presents

[The paper/ study/ article/ work…]

[Prior research/ investigations…]

·        evaluates

·        interprets

·        delvesinto

·        defines

·        dissects

·        probes

·        tests

·        explores

2概述课题的研究背景                           

 

2.1讨论一个课题的历史意义

 

[Subject/ Mechanism]

·        influences

·        controls

·        regulates

·        directs

·        inhibits

·        constrains

·        governs

·        plays an important in

·        plays a vital role in

 

2.2描述一个课题的受欢迎程度

 

·        Widelyaccepted, …

·        Thepreferred…

·        Commonly/Frequentlyimplemented,…

·        Theprevailing method for…

·        … is widely accepted as…

·        … is widely used as…

 

2.3描述一个课题最近引起了关注

 

·        Discussionsregarding … have dominated research in recent years.

·        …has appealed to…

·        …has propelled to the forefront in investigations of ...

·        …has dramatically/significantly shaped queries on … in recent years.

·        …has critically influenced academic dialogue on …

·        Much attention has been drawn to ...

·        ... has gained much importance in recent years

 

2.4确定当前关于某个课题的主流观点

 

·        Priorresearch generally confirms that…

·        Severalstudies agree that…

·        Priorresearch substantiates the belief that…

·        The consensus has been that…

 

2.5讨论已有文献的结论

 

[Previous studies]

·        contend

·        purport

·        suggest

·        evidence

2.6表达当前知识的广度或不足

 

·        Theacademic community has extensively explored X…

·        Priorresearch has thoroughly investigated….

·        However,little research has been conducted to show…

·        However,prior studies have failed to evaluate/ identify …

·        Much is known about…

·        But, little is knownabout…

 

2.7进一步表达你的研究问题

 

·        Recent/Previousstudies have promoted…

·        Priorinvestigations have implemented / queried diverse approaches to…

·        Anumber of authors have posited…

·        Several theories have been proposed to explain…

·        To solve this problem, many researchers have tried several methods
